The Accountant

  • Genre: Action | Crime | Drama
  • Language: English
  • Country of Origin: the USA
  • Release Date: 2016
  • Filming Locations: New York, the USA
  • Little Description: While for movie effect, the script calls out the repeated use of the number "three" as an indicator of fraudulent numbers, the theory behind fraudulent number detecion is known as "Benford's Law". The law states that in numbers, such as account transactions, the probability of a number occuring naturally drops, as one moves from smaller numbers to the larger numbers following a logarithmic scale. This law has been successfully used to detect fraudulent accounting transactions.
